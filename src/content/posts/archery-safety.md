This element is a substantial part of securing the archery course to guarantee that no one will get hurt from the vicinity as the arrow can flutter away and accidentally hit a person or an animal.

This refers to what extent you want your archery field to reach that seen as the “dead-end” crossover - where you adjust your bow strike to make your arrows more visible and reachable.
